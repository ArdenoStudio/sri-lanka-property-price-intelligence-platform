# Cold Outreach Templates — Phase 0 Interviews

**Goal:** Secure a 25–35 minute discovery call. NOT to sell. NOT to demo.
**Target response rate:** 20–30% on cold email with a warm signal (mutual connection, referral from another lawyer, specific recent case reference). Less on pure cold.
**Cadence:** initial send → wait 5 working days → one polite follow-up → stop. Do not triple-touch strangers; it burns the brand.

---

## Template A — Law firm, cold (partner/senior associate)

**Subject:** 20-minute call on conveyancing due diligence — research, not a pitch

> Dear [Mr./Ms. Surname],
>
> I'm building a property-intelligence service for Sri Lankan residential
> transactions and I'm currently interviewing ten conveyancing firms in Colombo
> before writing a single line of production code. [Firm name] kept coming up
> in my reading of recent reported cases as handling significant residential work,
> which is why I'm writing to you specifically.
>
> I have twelve structured questions about how your firm handles the
> market-price and risk checks that sit alongside title due diligence — what
> you do today, what slows the work down, what you wish existed. Twenty-five
> minutes, by phone or Zoom, whichever you prefer. I am not selling anything;
> if a product ever comes out of this research, I'll come back to you with a
> free sample report on one of your current deals so you can judge it on merit.
>
> Would any slot in the week of [date] work? Happy to fit around your calendar.
>
> Thank you for considering it.
>
> Kind regards,
> [Your name]
> [Phone]  |  [Email]
> [One-line bio: e.g. "Founder, Ardeno Studio — prior work in data engineering at X"]
