# DealScore — property.lk B&W mono

Ink / `--paper` only. **Never** green, amber, red, teal, clay, or emerald. Direction = pattern + weight + word — not hue.

## under / fair / over
| Word | Score | Meaning |
|------|------:|---------|
| under | `+10`…`+100` | Asking below similar |
| fair | `−9`…`+9` | Close to usual range |
| over | `−100`…`−10` | Asking above similar |

Collapse five-band detail copy to these three chrome words (full legend may keep nuance).
## Mono encoding
| | under | fair | over |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pattern | dense diagonal hatch | plain / hairline | sparse dots or dashed |
| Weight | bold 700 | regular muted | semibold 600 |
| Tick (opt.) | filled | hollow / em-dash | open outline |

List/compare: ink border, paper fill; under|over may invert — still mono. Detail meter: one ink track, center `0`; segments by pattern/opacity only. Prefer “below / typical / above similar”; avoid great/bad/underpriced/overpriced.
